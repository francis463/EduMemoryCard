Welcome to EduMemory!

Train your brain and test your knowledge with this fun and educational memory card game. Choose your difficulty level, match pairs, and challenge yourself to beat your best score!

Play Now How to Play

How to Play

  • Select your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).
  • Flip two cards — if they match, they stay revealed!
  • Try to match all pairs with the fewest attempts possible.
  • Use your hints wisely — you only get a few per round.
  • Track your accuracy and score as you play!
